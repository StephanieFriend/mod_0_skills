Beverage (Class)

Attributes:
alcoholic (boolean)
cold (boolean)
containerType (string)
currentVolume (integer)
name (string)

Methods:
addAlcohol (change alcoholic boolean to True)
addIce (change cold boolean to True)
requestToGo (change containerType to "plastic cup")
drink (subtract from currentVolume)
changeIngredient (changes the name of beverage)
